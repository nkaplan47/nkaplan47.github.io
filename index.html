Current Work

I am currently working at a Power Systems Software company called Open Systems International Inc., which specializes in software used to manage and optimize utility systems in all facets of the industry. (Link to company website here)

My work at the company varies from week to week, but a majority of work is done on the GIS(Geographic Information System) Extracts team. We specialize in converting the data from our new customer into an intermediate format that can be used within our software to represent their electric data model.

Another application I work on is a tool called Arrow that check standards laid out by our System Engineers. The tool can be deployed to multiple remote servers(windows or linux) and perform checks ranging from running local processes, to confirming databases are correctly configured.

I am also in charge of improvement and maintenance of what we called our Standards Website. It is a resource for our Project Engineers to give them a central location to view standards for our systems. The website is written is cshtml and implements an MVC design. It also uses a Microsoft SQL Server to store and add data for the website.

There are many other things I touch at OSI and I develop in multiple languages, so there is never a lack of variety!
